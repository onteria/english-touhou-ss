	From the day they are born humans are creatures of experimentation. Little by little the world around them unfolds, unveiling even more mysterious wonders. For those that realize this reality, everyday becomes more and more exciting. Alas, there are times when curiosity bears unusual desire. Such desire cannot be fulfilled by the everyday world around us. There are many rumors of a world hidden behind the cloak of reality. It sits and waits for the curious wanderer to pass upon it. What wonders await the lucky discoverer?
